Ordering Guidelines

Please read these guidelines carefully before placing an order. They explain stock status, item conditions, discounts, how ordering works, and what happens if you need to change or cancel an order.

How to order

  • Reach out to us on Instagram or WhatsApp to place an order. Join our WhatsApp community for stock alerts and special offers.
  • Private delivery only as of now. It can take up to 7–10 days to ship based on your order size so please be patient.

General

  • All items are authentic Bandai scale world 1/20 (unless otherwise specified), bought from Japan. You can verify markings even on the smallest Pokemon.
  • More pictures or real pictures (in case the catalog has reference image) can be shared upon request.
  • All items are in stock unless otherwise specified.
  • There is a small chance something is sold out but still listed. We will let you know before accepting payment.
  • Many of these are single or limited stock, so do not hesitate too much or you will miss out.
  • We can discard boxes if you do not need them but still want mint condition products.
  • We can sell you specific Pokémon from a set being sold, but that will be at a premium.

Item conditions

  • Missing Accessory/No Base – Items with missing accessories are priced accordingly. Many of them work fine without them. We can send you a video upon request.
  • Loose – The item does not have outer packaging, like a poly bag or a box. It has most likely been displayed before. We will mention any scratches or marks.
  • Sealed – The item is still in manufacturer packaging (plastic bag), but without a box. It has never been displayed before.
  • Box (opened) – The item comes with a box, but has previously been opened and likely displayed. More details will be provided in the notes.
  • Box (unopened) – The box may have been opened to verify the contents, but the items are still in a sealed plastic bag.

Discounts

  • Discounts are available on billing of multiple items. Your best offer will be shared with you on inquiry. There will also be limited time deals, auctions and more.
  • There are special offers for artists and people involved in the anime community (show us your cosplay or art, such as an Instagram page).

Order restrictions

  • Minimum order amount is 2000. Free shipping for orders over 5000. Flat fee of 150 for orders below 5000. This is fully up to our discretion and can change at any time.
  • If the order has been paid for but not packed:
    • More items can be added free of charge.
    • Items can be removed as long as you are reasonable.
    • Cancelling the order is possible as long as you do not keep changing your mind.
  • If the order has been packed but not shipped yet:
    • No new items can be added.
    • The order can be cancelled as a whole by paying a 10% fee of the entire order value.
    • Individual items can be removed by paying 10% of their value.
  • The fee gets deducted from your refund value in the above cases. More flexible for repeat customers.
  • If the order has been shipped, it is not possible to make any changes or cancel.
  • No returns if you change your mind.
  • In case of issues like missing, damaged, or incorrect items, an uncut unboxing video is required, and a refund will be issued. A return may or may not be required.
  • No COD; 100% payment will be upfront. If you are worried about scams, please share your concern.
