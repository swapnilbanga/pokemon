Ordering Guidelines

Please read these guidelines carefully before placing an order. They explain stock status, item conditions, discounts, how ordering works, and what happens if you need to change or cancel an order.

General

  • All items are authentic Bandai scale world 1/20 (unless otherwise specified), imported from Japan. You can verify markings even on the smallest Pokemon.
  • Most images will be reference images because actual products are in poly bags and I am too lazy to photograph them. I can send you real pictures before completing the order.
  • All items are in stock; none of them require booking or waiting.
  • All items are authentic Bandai scale world 1/20 (unless otherwise specified), imported from Japan.
  • There is a small chance something is sold out but still listed. I will let you know before accepting payment.
  • Many of these are single stock, so do not hesitate too much or you will miss out.
  • I generally do not accept requests like “bring me X or Y”. I can note your interest and let you know if I end up bringing it; the price may or may not be the same.
  • I can discard boxes if you do not need them but still want mint condition products.
  • I can sell you specific Pokémon from a set, but that will cost extra.

Item conditions

  • Missing Accessory – Sometimes, items might be missing a base or another accessory and they will be priced accordingly. Many of them work fine without them.
  • Open – The item does not have outer packaging, like a poly bag or a box. It has most likely been displayed before. These are generally in good or excellent condition, but if there are any scratches or marks I will mention them.
  • Sealed – The item is still in manufacturer packaging, but without a box. It has never been displayed before.
  • Boxed – The item comes with a box. Whether it has been opened before will be separately mentioned. In most cases it will be sealed too.

Discounts

  • Discounts are generally only possible if you place a large order and order a good ratio of trainers and Pokémon.
  • There are special offers for artists and people involved in the anime community (for example, show me your cosplay or art, such as an Instagram page).

How to order

  • DM me on WhatsApp with the number or name on the product cards.
  • Private delivery only as of now. It can take up to 7–10 days to ship based on your order size and my availability.

Order restrictions

  • Minimum order amount is 2500. Shipping will be divided between us and it will be nominal.
  • If the order has been paid for but not packed:
    • More items can be added free of charge.
    • Items can be removed as long as you are reasonable.
    • Cancelling the order is possible as long as you do not keep changing your mind.
  • If the order has been packed but not shipped yet:
    • No new items can be added.
    • The order can be cancelled as a whole by paying a 10% fee of the entire order value.
    • Individual items can be removed by paying 10% of their value.
  • The fee gets deducted from your refund value in the above cases. For repeat customers, I am more flexible.
  • If the order has been shipped, it is not possible to make any changes or cancel.
  • No returns if you change your mind.
  • In case of issues like missing, damaged, or incorrect items, an uncut unboxing video is required, and a refund will be issued. A return may or may not be required.
  • No COD; 100% payment will be upfront.
    • If you want, I can send you proof that I have no motivation or need to scam, but that is the most I can do.
